We are currently experiencing a cultural shift: the secularization of modern society. Navigating through life in this post-Christian culture can prove difficult. We believe Bridgetown church to be particularly attuned to the unique challenges that come with. It must be noted, however, that we do not seek to champion one church over another, as Emerson Eggerichs says "it's not wrong, just different." 1 While his words are admittedly taken out of context, they reveal a truth that accurately pertains to our multi-denominational Christianity.

The heart of Bridgetown church is the Missional Community. Bridgetown gatherings are only the "tip of the iceberg". Church is so much more than showing up on a Sunday to chant a few songs and hear a sermon. Bridgetown is called to minister to Portland neighborhoods.
